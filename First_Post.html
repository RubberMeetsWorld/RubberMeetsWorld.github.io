First Post

By: Garrett | Published: 2021-06-05

Some of what I want to share on this blog is documents of my travels and some projects that I am working on. I want a place to share some ideas and document where I have been and where I am planning to go.

So far there is not many places I can go right now. The one big cross country road trip that I had planned got killed when the covid-19 pandemic started, and I have been stuck in the same place ever since. Being stuck in one place wasn't inherently a bad thing, as I was able to dedicate time to other projects such as setting up this blog. While there were many terrible things that have happened during the pandemic I was trying to make the most out of being stationary.

I was lucky enough to keep my health up during the past year and I have picked up some responsibilities that are going to take up my time until school starts up again. It may be a while until I can do some extended travel, but thats ok. Not much to do except to enjoy where I currently am.
